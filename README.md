## The Problem

70% of US grocery products contain additives banned in the EU. Your "organic" brand is likely owned by a conglomerate that lobbies against food safety regulations. You have no way to know what's really in your food — until now.

## The Solution

6 AI agents that scan, grade, and expose what's in your food:

- **Product Grader** — A-F safety grade via Open Food Facts + EU banned additive database
- **Clean Swapper** — 3 alternatives for every flagged product (cleanest, budget, local)
- **Brand Unmasker** — reveals corporate ownership, lobbying spend, controversies
- **Recall Radar** — FDA recall monitoring with real-time alerts
- **Viral Content Engine** — Toxic Tuesday, Brand Exposé Friday, Swap Saturday
- **Local Sourcing** — farmers markets, CSAs, organic grocers near you

### Revenue Model (self-sustaining)
Affiliate commissions on clean alternatives fund the free tools:
- Thrive Market: 40%
- Daily Harvest: 15%
- Paleovalley: 25-40%

**You scan a toxic product → we show you a clean alternative → affiliate revenue funds the platform. Nobody pays. Everyone benefits.**
